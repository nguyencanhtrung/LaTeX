	\subsection{Golden Ration}
	Two quantities $a$ and $b$ are said to be in the \emph{golden ration $\varphi$}  if
	
	\begin{equation}
			\frac{a}{b} = \frac{a + b}{a} = \varphi
	\end{equation}
	
	By the way: $\varphi = 1 + \frac{1}{\varphi}$
